üí° Quick Tips

  • Search: Type in the search bar to find recipes by name, ingredients, or tags
  • Tags: Click on tags to filter recipes, or create custom tags in the Tags page
  • Favorites: Click the star icon to mark recipes as favorites
  • Shopping List: Add ingredients from recipes to your shopping list for easy meal planning
  • Rich Text: Use the formatting toolbar when editing recipes to add colors and styles
  • Import/Export: Back up your recipes by exporting them as JSON files
  • Themes: Customize the app's appearance with different color themes
  • Mobile: On touch devices, long-press recipe cards for quick actions

üéØ Getting Started

  1. Click the + button to add your first recipe
  2. Or use the "Try Sample Recipes" button to load examples
  3. Organize recipes with tags for easy filtering
  4. Build a shopping list from your favorite recipes
  5. Export your collection to back up your recipes
